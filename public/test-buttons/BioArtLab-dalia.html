Goals:
We seek to extend the conversation on sustainability to the arts. This project is integrated with sustainable agriculture to show the interdependence of systems and disprove the autonomy of the arts.

Issues Involved or Addressed:
  • Wastefulness in the arts
  • Sustainability
  • Renewable modes of production
Methods & Technologies:
  • Use of mycelium
  • SCOBY
  • Bioluminescent bacteria for use as artistic mediums
  • Use of plant matter for natural dye production
  • Composting

Preferred Majors for this Team:
Artists and art students and people working with conventional arts practices within the art market. Also anyone interested in changing their relationship to materiality is welcome as well.
